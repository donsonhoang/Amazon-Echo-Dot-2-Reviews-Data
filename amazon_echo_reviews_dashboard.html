What I Found

🎵
Music is the #1 use case
"Music" appears 1,291 times. "Speaker" and "sound" round out the top 10. Audio playback drives both the highest praise and the most complaints.
🔇
Speaker quality = low ratings
1- and 2-star reviews almost always mention the speaker being too quiet or tinny. The most common fix recommended: pair it with a Bluetooth speaker.
🏠
Smart home users are the loyalists
4- and 5-star reviewers frequently mention lights, multiple units, and home automation. These customers are the most likely to buy again.
⬛
Black outsells White 79% to 21%
5,410 Black vs. 1,445 White. Average ratings are nearly identical (4.21 vs 4.20), so color is a style choice — not a quality signal.
📈
September launch spike
4,726 reviews in September vs. 2,129 in October. The 2nd-gen Echo Dot launched Sep 2017, which explains the 2.2× surge in first-month reviews.
🔍
Unverified buyers rate lower
Only 3.1% of reviews are unverified, but they average 3.90★ vs 4.22★ for verified buyers. Could be gifted units, or review manipulation worth flagging.
