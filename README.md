Amazon Echo Dot 2 — Customer Review Analysis

Tools: MySQL, HTML/CSS/JavaScript
Dataset: 6,855 Amazon customer reviews

I scraped and cleaned customer review data from Amazon's Echo Dot 2 product pages and loaded it into MySQL. I then wrote queries to analyze rating distributions, sentiment patterns, and how factors like verified purchase status and product color influenced the reviews. The results were used to build an interactive dashboard exploring customer feedback trends over time.

Questions explored:


What is the overall rating distribution across all reviews?
Do verified purchasers rate the product differently than non-verified?
How do ratings vary by product color (Black vs. White)?
How has customer sentiment changed over time?
What words and phrases appear most in low vs. high rated reviews?
